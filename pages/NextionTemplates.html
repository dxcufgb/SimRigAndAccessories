NOTE: all dashboards are developed to be used with Simhub software developed by Wotever. You can find Simhub over at http://www.simhubdash.com!

1. Subaru WRX STI S207

This was my first (complete) attempt at a dashboard. the dashboard is mainly adjusted to work with Forza Motorsport 7 (FM7) and might therefore need a little tweaking to work with other games.

Since it’s based on a street car dashboard there’s not really a lot of values being presented except speed, rpm, boost(since I couldn’t find current consumption rate for FM7) current and total laps, position and ODO.
